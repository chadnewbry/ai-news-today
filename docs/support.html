Common Questions

How do I enable daily news notifications?

Go to the Settings tab and toggle on "Daily News Alert." You can also choose your preferred delivery time. If notifications are disabled at the system level, the app will prompt you to enable them in your device settings.

Why am I not receiving notifications?

Make sure notifications are enabled both in the app (Settings tab) and in your device's system settings (Settings → Notifications → ai news today). Also check that Do Not Disturb or Focus modes aren't blocking alerts.

How do I change the digest delivery time?

In the Settings tab, tap the time picker under "Daily News Alert" to select your preferred time.

Is the app free?

Yes, ai news today is completely free to use.
